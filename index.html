Why Choose Us

Recruit IT personnel

Quality management

We are building a quality control system led by Japanese Bridge SE who has abundant experience in offshore development. In addition, we are actively conducting in-house quality awareness education.

Offshore Development

We conduct offshore development at overseas development bases.

Offshore development refers to outsourcing software development, Web system development, smartphone application development, Facebook application development, social game development, and operation and maintenance management to overseas development companies and overseas subsidiaries.

This method is used to secure resources and reduce development costs. In recent years, it has been adopted as a base for securing resources and R & D.

In addition to the usual manufacturing contracts, there are lab contracts that can secure and secure excellent human resources.

Our unique strength
  • Japanese SE can be selected as a contact
  • Actively employs excellent engineers who can speak Japanese
  • Appropriate human resources can be assigned in cooperation with each of our bases (our bases: Tokyo, Vietnam, Myanmar, Philippines)
